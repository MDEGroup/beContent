What are RSS feeds?

RSS (Really Simple Syndication) feeds provide free content from Web sites. The feeds from the Model Transformation site include headlines and links back to the site for the full contents.

What do I need to use RSS?

You need a special news reader that displays this content from sites you select. These readers are either a piece of downloadable software or a Web-based browser. A list of readers can be found on google.com.
